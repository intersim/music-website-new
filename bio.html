Emily Intersimone is a composer and songwriter from Santa Cruz, California. She is currently based in New York City.

Composing: jazz composition, songwriting, music for theatre.

Music for Theatre: music director, associate music director, arranger/orchestrator

Teaching background: NYU adjunct instructor, USC JazzReach, choir director and private studio in Santa Cruz

Gigging jazz pianist

Education: She earned her Bachelor of Music degree in Jazz Piano from the University of Southern California, and her Master of Music degree in Jazz Composition from New York University. She has studied with Grammy-winning and -nominated jazz musicians such as Russell Ferrante (The Yellow Jackets), Kenny Werner, and Gil Goldstein (Esperanza Spalding, Michael Brecker), and film composer Ira Newborn (Ace Ventura: Pet Detective, Ferris Bueller's Day Off).
