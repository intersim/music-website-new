Lesson Policies

  • Lessons take place at the student's home.
  • The first lesson is always a free trial lesson.
  • After the first lesson, lessons will be paid one month at a time at the first lesson of the month. An invoice will be sent via email with the dates of the month's lessons, so you may plan accordingly.
  • You may pay for lessons with cash, check, or PayPal (a 10% service charge will apply)
  • Cancellation policy: if a lesson is cancelled with a notice of 24 hours or more, a makeup lesson may be scheduled OR a credit will be added to the next invoice. If a lesson is cancelled with less than 24 hours notice, there will be no credit for the lesson.
  • Material costs are in addition to the cost of lessons. Most lesson books are under $10.
